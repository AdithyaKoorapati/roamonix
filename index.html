Get ready to revolutionize the way street lights are managed and powered with Romanix Technologies!

Currently, street lights account for a significant 20% of the electricity consumed by the Indian government. The existing system relies on linemen and foremen to manually inspect and maintain each stretch of street lights, resulting in inefficiencies and costly maintenance. Coordinating with assistant engineers and district engineers from the substations further adds complexity to the process. This has led to a staggering 46% of street lights nationwide being inadequately maintained, placing a heavy financial burden on the government. But fear not, because at Romanix Technologies, we have developed a groundbreaking IoT instrument that will transform the entire street light management paradigm.

Our innovative solution involves integrating this device at every meter on every street, enabling seamless automation and control from anywhere. With our mobile software, linemen, foremen, and district engineers gain full control over the street lights, allowing them to adjust brightness, access crucial statistics, and optimize overall efficiency with just a few taps.

This breakthrough technology not only saves the government billions of rupees, but it also provides a smarter, more efficient, and easier solution to an age-old problem. By embracing our cutting-edge solution, you can be at the forefront of a transformative movement that eliminates unnecessary costs, streamlines operations, and enhances the overall functionality of street lighting infrastructure.

Join forces with Romanix Technologies today and be part of a revolution that brings intelligent automation to the heart of your city's streets. Together, let's create a future where street light management is cost-effective, energy-efficient, and hassle-free, paving the way for smarter and more sustainable communities.
